How to find your booking ?

1. Enter your Booking Reference Number. It must be a confirmation number consisting of alphabets and numbers on the top right corner of your ticket. Please note that the reference number is case sensitve.

2. Enter your Last name as you entered while making the booking.

3. Enter your Phone number registed with us. It is that number which you provided while making the booking.

On pressing the Find my booking button, you will be showed options such as :

  • Cancel your reservation
  • Resend the Ticket via SMS / Whatsapp / Email
  • Print your ticket
